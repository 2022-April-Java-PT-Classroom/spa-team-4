Create Your Own Art!

How to make art:

  1. Select a color by clicking on one of the color squares above the sketch box.
  2. Click inside the box to create a colored square.
  3. Repeat until you've made a masterpiece!

How to save your image:

  1. After finishing your art, press WIN + SHIFT + S (COMMAND + SHIFT + 5 for Mac users).
  2. Hold your mouse down at the top left of the art and drag it toward the bottom right.
  3. Release to copy the selected area and save the image!
